World First Shadowlands +24 Mythic Dungeon Completed In Time

 A huge congratulations to the Vengeance Demon Hunter Anuodh, Holy Paladin 麒丨麟丶, Fire Mage 丶清心, Balance Druid Yomamadr , and Outlaw Rogue 小红手叶落 for finishing +24 Necrotic Wake in 34 minutes and 44 seconds - the world first +24 Mythic Dungeon of Shadowlands Season One to be completed within time! Composition wise, virtually all of the top keys use Demon Hunter tanks due to the extra utility they provide over other tanks, and although Holy Paladin is less common than Restoration Shaman it's still fairly well represented at the high end. Balance Druids, Fire Mages, and Outlaw Rogues are very common DPS, so nothing out of the ordinary there aside from excellent execution.
