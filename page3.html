Representation Changes from Previous Week

Overall, class and spec representation remained the same when compared to last week, with the large outliers for Marksmanship Hunter, Fire Mage, Balance Druid, Vengeance Demon Hunter, and Restoration Shaman. Besides those five specs, Outlaw Rogue, Holy Paladin, and Windwalker Monk have decent representation in 15s and their representation gets increased when looking at +20 or higher keys. Every other spec sees a reduction in usage as you go higher in keys, showing the prevalence of these eight specs in the Mythic+ meta. You can read more about the Mythic+ Meta in Shadowlands Season 1 in our in-depth analysis.

Below you can see snapshots of the charts. You can see their full interactive versions.

Volume of Keys - Fortified vs Tyrannical

The largest change between the weeks is the volume of keys completed. With 143 thousand completed in time for level 15 and up during the Fortified week, an increase of over 50% compared to the previous Tyrannical week. There's even a starker contrast when looking at level 20 and up dungeons, with only 98 being completed during the Tyrannical week, compared to almost 2000 timed keys during the Fortified week, a perfect example of the discrepancy between the affixes in high keys.
